BATTLE GALACTICA

Navigate through the cosmic tunnel and destroy enemy targets!

Use your mouse to aim and click to fire your limited laser ammo.

Destroy targets to earn points and replenish ammo.

MISSION BRIEFING

OBJECTIVE:

Destroy as many enemy targets as possible while navigating through the cosmic tunnel.

CONTROLS:

  • Move your mouse to aim the crosshair
  • Left click to fire your laser
  • ESC key to pause the game

GAME MECHANICS:

  • You start with limited ammo (10 shots)
  • Each destroyed target gives you +1 ammo
  • Each hit scores 100 points
  • Missed shots don't replenish ammo
  • Game speed increases as you progress
